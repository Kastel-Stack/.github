# Code of Conduct

Kastel Stack spaces are for clear, rigorous, good-faith collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- treat others with respect
- critique ideas and implementation details directly, not people
- avoid harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct
- protect confidential or private information
- disclose conflicts of interest when relevant

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or participants that undermine constructive collaboration or create safety, privacy, or legal risk.
